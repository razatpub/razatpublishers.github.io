who we are

Razat Publishers Limited is a book publishing firm that is dedicated to helping children build their future through its collaboration with sound and intelligent authors to print quality and highly educative books.

We have continued to scale high in the production of quality materials as well as leisure/story books.

We print high quality textbooks for children in nursery and primary schools. We prioritise the needs to aid better learning of our children, thereby providing excellent design prints.

Our editorial standards are meticulous: our design is clear and elegant, and more importantly, we work based on the new curriculum provided by the government
